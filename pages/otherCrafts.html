Trollskull Manor

This Project was hand built from cardboard, popsicle sticks, and cardstock. It's based on the Trollskull Manor from Waterdeep dragon heist. My favorite part of this project is still the roof, the green, and black really stand out and It gives some color to something that's otherwise very drab.

Cerberus Airship

This airship is for a D&D character I played from level 1 to level 20, their name is Aero and they're a half-elf multiclass thingy. This airship model is another old project, the design on the sail leaves a lot to be desired but other than that I'm very proud of it. The planks of wood are mostly cardstock, but on the deck, I used popsicle sticks. I love to drybrush and this project was perfect for that!
