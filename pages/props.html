Rapier

a sword made from a combonation of a couple thingiverse models. I love how it came out but its not one of the things im most proud of. The blade was designed my me but the handle and rose were combined into it using meshmixer. Im very happy with the finish on the blade and I used a steel rod for the core, which makes it fun to swing around!

Lightsaber

Custom lightsaber for my friend as a Christmas gift! It was my first big project on the lathe and it turned out ok. I'm much more confident that I can machine stuff now but I do wish I had more time to add more details. It is powered by a flashlight and turns on by pressing a button at the bottom of the hilt. The blade is from Vire Sabers. If I was doing this again I would probably just buy the blade tip from them and then get a piece of tube from somewhere else.
