Custom Box Car

A custom box car made using a menards box car, some paint, and a UV printer for the stickets. It was a really fun project and I would love to work on more like them in the future. They do take quite a bit of time due to all the different stages of painting but I think it's worth it. I was told by some people on a model train form that the registration numbers should be on the left side- oops!

I would be happy to make these for other people, but if the image supplied is too tall then it will need to be cut down, fair warning!
